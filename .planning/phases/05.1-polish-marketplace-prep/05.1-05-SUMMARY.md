## What Was Built

The "Threat Model Notes" section of `SECURITY.md` was rewritten to explicitly separate three distinct trust boundaries that the original single paragraph conflated:

- **Stage 1 — Extension code:** Zero outbound HTTP, zero telemetry, zero remote execution. Verified in CI by `scripts/check-no-network.mjs`.
- **Stage 2 — Extension to local Discord client (IPC):** Unix domain socket on macOS/Linux (`/tmp/discord-ipc-N`), named pipe on Windows. Local to user account, does not traverse a network interface.
- **Stage 3 — Local Discord client to Discord's servers:** The local Discord client forwards the activity payload to Discord's servers — this is how Rich Presence works. Controlled by Discord, governed by [Discord's privacy policy](https://discord.com/privacy). Not controlled by this extension.

The companion-detector lockfile paragraph (post-WR-01 wording) was preserved byte-identical.
